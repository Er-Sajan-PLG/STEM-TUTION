❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects do you teach? ▼
We teach Mathematics and Science for Grade 1–10, and Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics for Grade 11–12, aligned with the Nepali national curriculum (SEE and NEB).
How do I enroll my child? ▼
Simply call or WhatsApp us at +977 9768021317 or email us. We'll discuss your child's grade and needs, then assign them to the right batch. There's no complicated process!
What are the batch timings? ▼
We currently run three batches: Early Morning (6–8 AM), After-School (4–6 PM), and Evening (6–8 PM). Contact us to confirm which slots are currently available for your grade.
What is the fee structure? ▼
Fees vary by grade and subject combination. Please contact us directly for the latest fee structure. We believe education should be accessible and are open to discussing options.
How many students are in each batch? ▼
We keep batches small on purpose — so every student gets personal attention, can ask questions freely, and doesn't get left behind.
Do you provide study materials? ▼
Yes — notes, practice problems, and past exam papers are provided as part of the class. We also have a free YouTube channel with supplementary video lessons at youtube.com/@LearningHubSTEM.
