📝 How to Add Your YouTube Videos Here

1. Go to any video on your YouTube channel.
2. Click Share → Embed and copy the src URL — it looks like https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID
3. In this file (videos.html), find a placeholder card and replace the <div class="video-placeholder"> block with:

<div class="video-embed"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_VIDEO_ID" allowfullscreen></iframe></div>

4. Update the title, subject, description and tags. Save the file and re-upload to Netlify. Done! 🎉
